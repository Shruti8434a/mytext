About me

DEVASHISH KUMAR

Founder, CodroidHub

AWS Certified Solutions Architect- Associate and Student of FITT, IIT Delhi. Mr. Devashish Kumar is Pursuing Ph.D. in CSE. With more than 5+ years of experience as an Assistant Professor, Mr. Kumar has been recognized several times for his efforts and contribution towards the inclusion of applied work among students. Mr. Kumar has multiple certification in MOOCs courses conducted by Stanford University, Johns Hopkins University, IIT Kanpur at Coursera, Edx, NPTEL. Appreciated by NPTEL for his instrumental role as Mentor and SPOC, Mr. Kumar guided more than 70+ MOOCs and 100+ students in NPTEL course. Few of his students brought laurels by figuring in the top 5 positions in such courses. Mr. Kumar has also mentored students at National Level Hackathonâ€™s and Coding Events conducted by Smart India Hackathon, ACM and guided 15 real-time projects in Android, Python, Machine Learning available on Google PlayStore and GIT. It also improves the skills of Educators/Faculty members by conducting FDPs on recent technologies.
